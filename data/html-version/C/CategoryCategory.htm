[edit]Organized Entries

This is a list of categories created for the NobleWiki¬. The WikiNames¬ for the categories all begin with a Category prefix. The purpose of each category entry is to group related information. Other prefixes are available, and can also be created in order to organize custom information.

General Purpose
CategoryOverview FAQs, TourBusStop, and other information related to the goals of this wiki
CategoryWiki How to use a wiki, and bunches of wiki-related terms
CategoryAdmin Useful information for NobleWiki¬ adminisrators
CategoryPeople Lists of persons contributing, developing, or related to any aspect of this wiki.

Site-centric Purpose
CategoryGames Lists of all games grouped by their purpose, with links to their own subsections.
CategoryIllustration Tutorials on using graphics design tools
CategoryWebDev  Items of interests for W/LAMP web development; HTML/CSS, javascript, perl, MySql
CategoryGalleries  Image galleries of various sorts, including those associated with games
CategoryLinks  Useful bookmarks and links to other sites
CategoryMisc  A bucket category to catch small things until a separate category can be created.
CategoryTemplates  A list of all templates and transclusion files
